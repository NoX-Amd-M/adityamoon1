After Class 10

Six paths. One choice that feels permanent. (It isn't.)

India offers more than the usual Science/Commerce/Arts split. Here's the full picture, including vocational and diploma routes most students never hear about.

01 / Science

Science (PCM / PCB)

Physics · Chemistry · Maths or Biology
  • Engineering, medicine, architecture, research
  • Keeps commerce & most arts options open later
  • Good fit if: you like solving problems, not just memorising
Read the full guide →
02 / Commerce

Commerce

Accountancy · Economics · Business Studies
  • CA, CS, banking, business, economics, management
  • Strong base for entrepreneurship later
  • Good fit if: numbers and "how businesses work" interest you
Read the full guide →
03 / Arts / Humanities

Arts / Humanities

History · Political Science · Psychology · Languages
  • Law, civil services, design, journalism, psychology
  • Widest range of electives to mix and match
  • Good fit if: you're drawn to people, society, or ideas
Read the full guide →
04 / Vocational / ITI

Vocational & ITI

Electrician · Fitter · IT · Hospitality trades
  • Industrial Training Institutes — 1–2 year trade certificates
  • Fastest route to a paying technical job
  • Good fit if: you want hands-on skills and quicker employment
Read the full guide →
05 / Diploma

Polytechnic Diploma

Mechanical · Civil · Electronics · Computer
  • 3-year diploma straight after 10th, skips 11th–12th
  • Can later join engineering via lateral entry
  • Good fit if: you already know you want a technical career
Read the full guide →
06 / Defence Prep

Defence-Track Schools

Sainik School · Military School · NCC
  • Builds toward NDA, CDS, or armed forces entry after 12th
  • Physical training alongside regular academics
  • Good fit if: you're drawn to discipline and service
Read the full guide →
After Class 12

What each stream actually leads to

Read it like a timetable: your stream is the row, the period is how far it can take you.

Stream Common courses Entrance exams Typical duration Where it leads
Science (PCM) B.Tech, B.Arch, B.Sc, Integrated M.Sc JEE Main/Advanced, CET, NATA 4 yrs Engineering, architecture, data science, research
Science (PCB) MBBS, BDS, B.Sc Nursing, B.Pharm NEET 4–5.5 yrs Medicine, dentistry, allied health, pharma
Commerce B.Com, BBA, CA, CS, CMA CA Foundation, CUET, college-specific 3–5 yrs Finance, accounting, banking, business
Arts / Humanities BA (Hons), Law (5-yr), Journalism, Psychology CUET, CLAT, design/journalism entrances 3–5 yrs Law, civil services, media, social sciences, design
Vocational / ITI Advanced trade certificates, apprenticeships Usually no entrance — merit-based 1–2 yrs Skilled technical jobs, self-employment, PSU apprenticeships
Polytechnic Diploma Diploma engineering, lateral B.Tech entry State CET/polytechnic entrance 3 yrs (+2 lateral) Junior engineer roles, technical supervisor, further B.Tech
Defence NDA, Indian Military Academy, naval/air wings NDA (UPSC), CDS 3–4 yrs training Army, Navy, Air Force officer roles
Performing & Fine Arts BFA, BPA, design & film institutes NIFT, NID, FTII, institute-specific 3–4 yrs Design, film, animation, performing arts, fashion
